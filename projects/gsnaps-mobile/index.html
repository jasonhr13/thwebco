About Us

August 2007 changed everything...

I remember coming out of his office saying "It isn't a dream anymore... It's becoming a reality." Almost everyone who knows me knew I've always wanted my own "coffee shop". That was a passion of mine that I prayed about for many years, until one day... During my mothers daily prayer time she recalls hearing "The Open Door" - Not sure why, she prayed for the owners, for their business and then it occured to her, maybe this is for Lisa? Coming to me one day she says... "Have you ever considered asking the owners if they'd consider allowing you to put your coffee shop inside the bookstore?" The answer of course was no; but what could it hurt to ask? So...off I went. Praying about the "meeting". I went in and sat at his desk telling all about my ideas and my moms prayer all the time to a smiling man who I seriously thought he was thinking I was crazy! After all the plans were on the table he speaks, "Let me tell you where I'm at, I've been working with a redesigning firm, and they just suggested that I should put a coffee shop inside my bookstore." Thus Gingersnaps Coffeehouse and Cafe was born.

Lisa Eilbracht - Owner
