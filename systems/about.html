About Us

About us

The Systems House has been serving the Wabash Valley since 1983. We are locally owned and operated and take pride in the work we do for valley residents and businesses. We are continually striving to improve our service offerings to our clients. We have experienced electronic technicians, computer technicians and certified systems engineers on staff, including Microsoft Certfied Systems Engineers, Microsoft Certified Technology Specialists, A+ Technicians, Network+ Technicians.

Who are the owners?

The Systems House is owned by Jerry and Libby Wyrick. Having started the company over two decades ago, they remain committed to bringing the best of class service and support to the Wabash Valley.
